Who We Are

RAK News is an independent, reader-supported newsroom founded on a simple commitment: deliver the day's most important stories with truth, speed and integrity. We report from over sixty countries, serve more than four million readers each month, and answer to no one but our audience.

Our Values

Truth

Every story is verified by at least two independent sources. Corrections are published prominently and quickly when we get something wrong.

Speed

We break news fast — but never faster than we can confirm it. Our digital desk operates around the clock from three time zones.

Integrity

We accept no advertising from sources we cover. Our editorial team operates independently from commercial operations.
